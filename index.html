THE PROBLEM

The campus print shop hasn't changed in twenty years.

Every student knows the ritual: rush to the shop before class, wait behind a stack of assignments, pay cash, hope the operator shows up. It breaks down exactly when it matters most — deadline week.

01 · TIME

Long queues, worst timing

Shops are understaffed right when assignment and exam deadlines hit hardest — the moment students can least afford to wait.

02 · ACCESS

Fixed, daytime-only hours

No help for the 11pm print emergency, the Sunday deadline, or a holiday submission. If the shop's shut, you're stuck.

03 · PRIVACY

Your files, someone else's hands

Resumes, ID proofs, and project reports get handed over on a USB stick — with zero guarantee of what happens to the copy.

04 · PAYMENTS

Cash-only, receipt-less

No UPI, no digital trail, no way to dispute a bad print. Just loose change and good faith.

05 · RELIABILITY

One person, one point of failure

If the operator is late, sick, or simply distracted, printing stops for the entire campus. There's no fallback.

06 · VISIBILITY

No tracking, no history

No preview before printing, no job status, no record of what you printed last week. Mistakes just get re-paid for.

HOW IT WORKS

Five steps. Zero human in the loop.

TimeToPrint works like a printer on demand — you bring the file, the kiosk brings the printout.

01

Scan

Point your phone at the kiosk's QR code. No app install — it opens straight in your browser.

02

Upload

Send a PDF, DOCX, PNG or JPEG from your phone or cloud drive, then set copies, colour and duplex.

03

Schedule

Print now, or pick a slot that dodges the peak-hour rush entirely.

04

Pay

Settle instantly with UPI — GPay, PhonePe, Paytm. No cash, digital receipt every time.

05

Collect

Get a WhatsApp/SMS nudge when it's ready, then pick it up from a secure output tray.

WHAT MAKES IT NOVEL

Not a Xerox shop with a QR sticker on it.

TimeToPrint pairs a cloud submission platform with an autonomous kiosk — a combination no Indian print provider currently offers end-to-end.

Contactless vending

Users never interact with a person — from upload to pickup, the whole job is self-serve.

Live queue & slot view

See real-time wait times before you upload, and book a slot that actually avoids the rush.

Smart format detection

Auto-detects file type, page count and orientation, and flags low-resolution content before you pay.

Remote dashboard

Kiosk owners monitor usage, refill paper and ink, and troubleshoot — all without an on-site visit.

Offline-safe queuing

Jobs queue locally if the network drops, then sync the moment connectivity returns.

Auto-delete by design

No stored file outlives its print job — privacy isn't a setting, it's the default.

TECHNOLOGY STACK

Simple hardware, lightweight software.

Built to be repairable and cheap to replicate — not a proprietary black box.

Hardware

  • Raspberry Pi 4 — controller unit
  • USB/Bluetooth inkjet or laser printer
  • 7-inch touchscreen or QR poster display
  • Wi-Fi dongle + UPS power backup
  • Lockable metal/wood kiosk enclosure

Software

  • Web/app frontend + cloud backend
  • Razorpay / UPI payment gateway
  • Admin dashboard for remote monitoring
  • WhatsApp / SMS notification API
PRIVACY & MAINTENANCE

Your files aren't the product.

Documents pass through TimeToPrint — they don't stay there.

  • Encrypted uploads

    Files travel over encrypted connections from your phone to the kiosk.

  • Auto-delete on a timer

    Documents are wiped within minutes of a successful print, or after a short expiry window.

  • No operator access

    There's no person in the loop who can open, copy, or forward your file.

  • Secure payment APIs

    Razorpay/UPI handle payment directly — TimeToPrint never stores card or bank details.

  • Weekly consumable refills

    Paper and ink levels are checked on a fixed schedule, sized to campus volume.

  • Remote diagnostics

    Most issues are caught from the dashboard, cutting down on on-site visits.

  • Tamper-resistant enclosure

    A lockable case discourages casual theft or interference with internals.

  • Proactive alerts

    Low-paper, low-ink and jam errors trigger a WhatsApp/SMS alert automatically.
